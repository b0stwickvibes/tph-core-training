# 30-DAY EVALUATION RUBRIC
**Three Points Hospitality — C.O.R.E. Training System**
**Universal Criteria + Role-Specific Scoring**
**Last Updated: March 16, 2026**

---

## PURPOSE

The 30-Day Evaluation is conducted 30 days after a trainee's first solo shift. It is not a surprise audit — trainees are told during onboarding that this evaluation happens. The goal is to verify that training translated into consistent floor performance, not just checklist completion.

Completed jointly by the GM and the trainee's primary trainer.

---

## SCORING SYSTEM

| Score | Label | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **3** | Meets Standard | Executes consistently without prompting |
| **2** | Developing | Executes but inconsistently or with coaching |
| **1** | Below Standard | Does not execute reliably; requires intervention |
| **N/O** | Not Observed | Could not be evaluated during this review period |

---

## OUTCOME THRESHOLDS

| Outcome | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **PASS** | Total score 85% or above of available points; no category scored all 1s |
| **CONDITIONAL PASS** | Total score 70-84%; coaching plan issued; re-evaluated at 60 days |
| **NEEDS IMPROVEMENT** | Total score below 70%; or any category with all 1s; re-training required |

*Available points = total scored categories x 3. N/O scores are excluded from the denominator.*

## TRAINING CHECKLIST

### 1. Opening Checklist
- [ ] Trainee performs opening checklist via Jolt app with trainer guiding
- [ ] FOH setup: table settings, condiments, silverware, section readiness
- [ ] "Your section should be guest-ready before the first table sits down."

### 2. Service Sequence Review
- [ ] Full walkthrough: greeting, drink order, appetizer suggestion, entrée order, check-back, dessert/coffee offer, check presentation, farewell
- [ ] Practice proper table approach, body language, and timing at an empty table
- [ ] Consistency in the sequence is what separates reliable service from guesswork

### 3. Trainer Support & Guest Engagement
- [ ] Trainee begins taking orders, answering guest questions, handling requests under trainer supervision
- [ ] Learn to manage multiple tables while maintaining attentiveness
- [ ] Trainer coaches in real time, stepping in only when necessary

### 4. Menu Training
- [ ] Review key menu items: ingredients, preparation methods, guest favorites, seasonal specials
- [ ] Discuss high-margin items and how to describe them confidently
- [ ] "A deep understanding of the menu makes you a trusted resource for guests."

### 5. Review of Food Allergies
- [ ] Common allergens: nuts, dairy, gluten, shellfish, soy, eggs
- [ ] Severity of allergic reactions and cross-contamination prevention
- [ ] "If you don't know what's in a dish, ask the kitchen. Never guess."

### 6. Review of Dietary Options
- [ ] Dietary restrictions: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, keto
- [ ] Know which menu items cater to each and what substitutions are available
- [ ] Being prepared with answers makes service smoother for everyone

### 7. Beverage Service Basics
- [ ] Overview of full bar offerings: liquor, beer, wine, signature cocktails, non-alcoholic options
- [ ] Age verification procedures and responsible alcohol service
- [ ] Pairing suggestions: what drinks complement which dishes

### 8. Table Management & Pacing
- [ ] Techniques for managing multiple tables without dropping quality
- [ ] Prioritize by urgency: who just sat, who's waiting on food, who needs a check
- [ ] Awareness of table status across your section at all times

### 9. Teamwork & Communication
- [ ] Importance of pre-shift meetings and ongoing communication during service
- [ ] Coordinating with kitchen, bartenders, food runners, and fellow servers
- [ ] "Strong communication leads to fewer mistakes and better guest service."

### 10. Upselling Techniques
- [ ] Introduction to upselling: suggest add-ons, premium options, desserts, drink pairings
- [ ] Practice with trainer using real menu scenarios
- [ ] "It's not about pushing product. It's about reading what the guest wants and offering something better."

### 11. Service Skills Practice
- [ ] Continue working with trainer: take orders, run food, pre-bus, interact with guests
- [ ] Refine service etiquette, pacing, and handling guest needs
- [ ] Gradually take on more responsibility as confidence builds

### 12. Toast POS Practice
- [ ] Advanced functions: split checks, manage tabs, handle special requests, coursing
- [ ] Trainee rings in live orders under trainer supervision
- [ ] Troubleshoot common issues: printer jams, duplicate tickets, voiding items
- [ ] "Speed on Toast comes from knowing where everything is. Practice until it's muscle memory."

# Server Training Checklist

---

## 🟢 Before We Begin

### Trainer: Verify the following before every training shift.

**Day 1 — Confirm pre-training modules:**
- [ ] "READ ME: Welcome to Trainual" module completed
- [ ] Employee Handbook Review + Verification Test completed
- [ ] Server Manual reviewed
- [ ] Core Materials covering procedures, service expectations, and safety completed

**Days 2–5 — Confirm previous day's work:**
- [ ] Previous day's Knowledge Check completed in Trainual
- [ ] Previous day's Training Session reviewed
- [ ] Assigned materials reviewed (menu sections, handbook sections, etc.)

**If any items are incomplete:**
Pause training. Guide the trainee to complete outstanding items via Trainual. Log the delay in the Accountability App.

**Verify understanding** by asking 2–3 targeted questions from the previous day's material before proceeding.

## 🏁 End of Shift

### Trainer: Complete the following before clocking out.

**Checklist signed?**
- [ ] All checklist items checked
- [ ] Trainee signature obtained
- [ ] Trainer signature completed
- [ ] MOD signature obtained (Day 1 only)

**Submit your Accountability Report:**

| IMPORTANT: Fill this out at the end of every trainer shift. |
| :---: |

1. Fill out all required fields accurately.
2. Be specific when assessing your trainee's performance.
3. Submit before ending your shift.

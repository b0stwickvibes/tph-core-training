# SECTION 1: WHY WE'RE HERE

## The Problem

The C.O.R.E. content is solid. The 5-day structure works. Trainual works. None of that is the issue.

**The issue is execution at the trainer level:**

- Accountability forms aren't getting filled out consistently
- End-of-shift recaps get skipped when it gets busy
- Zero visibility into what's actually being taught vs. what's being checked off
- No structured touchpoint between management and trainers during the 5-day window
- The 30-day mentorship period exists on paper only

**The result:** Inconsistent trainee quality. Some come out sharp, some need re-training within weeks. The variable isn't the program. It's the trainer. We're fixing that.

---

## What's NOT Changing

- 5-day training structure (Bartender, Server, Host)
- Trainual platform and knowledge checks
- Mock Service Examination and final evaluation
- The training content itself (topics, curriculum, day-by-day progression)

**We're fixing the execution layer, not rebuilding the house.**

# SECTION 2: WHAT'S NEW

## The Three-Layer Document System

We've moved from two overlapping documents (Trainer Manual + Trainee Manual) to three purpose-built layers. Each one does one job.

| Layer | Who Uses It | What It Is | Status |
|-------|------------|------------|--------|
| **Trainer Manual** | Trainers only | Teaching reference. Talking points, demos, coaching methodology. Used during retraining and as a reference. | ✅ Existing (cleaned up) |
| **Daily Floor Checklist** | Trainer + Trainee (on shift) | Printed one-pager per day. Checkbox format. Carry it, check it off, sign it, photograph it, submit it. | ✅ NEW — 15 built |
| **Enriched Trainee Manual** | Trainee (post-shift) | Study guide in Trainual. Explains the "what" and "why" behind every topic practiced on the floor. Makes knowledge checks meaningful. | ✅ NEW — 3 roles built |

### How They Work Together

```
BEFORE SHIFT         DURING SHIFT              AFTER SHIFT
─────────────        ──────────────────        ──────────────────
Trainer reviews      Trainer walks through     Trainer submits
Trainer Manual       printed checklist         Accountability Report
for talking points   step by step              via the App
                     ↓                         ↓
                     Both sign checklist        Trainee completes
                     at end of shift            Knowledge Check
                                               in Trainual
                                               ↓
                                               Trainee reviews
                                               Enriched Manual
                                               to reinforce learning
```

## The Daily Training Guide (NEW Trainual Subject)

Each role now has a **Daily Training Guide** subject in Trainual with 3 steps:

| Step | Name | What It Does |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Step 1** | 🟢 Before We Begin | Trainer verifies trainee completed previous day's Knowledge Check and assigned materials. If incomplete, pause and log it. |
| **Step 2** | 📋 Daily Floor Checklists | Links to all 5 days' printable checklists (Google Sheets + PDF). Trainer prints the correct day and uses it on the floor. |
| **Step 3** | 🏁 End of Shift | Checklist sign-off verification + direct link to Accountability App. Next steps communicated to trainee. |

**This replaces** the accountability section, trainee checklist section, and trainee accountability check that were previously embedded inside the Trainer Manual. One location for shift operations. No duplication.

---

## The Enriched Trainee Manual (NEW in Trainual)

**Before:** Trainee Manual had 1-2 sentence topic descriptions. Trainee goes home with no real study material.

**Now:** Every training step includes structured reinforcement content using one of three templates:

| Template | Used When | Structure |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Skill** | Physical execution (pouring, shaking, building) | Spec → Metrics → Pro Tip → Common Misses |
| **Process** | Systems and workflow (POS, setup, closing) | Objective → Audit → Reset → Result |
| **Guest-Facing** | Guest interaction (greeting, upselling, complaints) | The Approach → Energy → Scenarios → Pitfalls |

**Result:** Trainee studies real substance at home. Knowledge checks test actual retention, not topic recognition.

---

## Accountability Enforcement (UPDATED)

| What | Rule |
|------|------|
| **Accountability Report** | Submitted via the App before the trainer leaves the building. Every shift. No exceptions. |
| **Checklist Photo** | Photo of the signed daily floor checklist uploaded with the report. |
| **No submission = no incentive** | Miss the form + photo? Miss the pay for that shift. Non-negotiable. |
| **Day 1 Check-In** | Management sits with the trainer for 10-15 min. Set the tone. Catch red flags early. |
| **Day 5 Check-In** | Management confirms the trainee is actually ready for mock service. Trainer's honest read. |
| **Days 2-4** | The form and checklist photo carry it. Red flags in submissions trigger early intervention. |

# SECTION 6: 30-DAY POST-TRAINING MENTORSHIP

| Phase | When | Trainer Does | Management Does |
|-------|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Training** | Days 1-5 | Executes daily checklist. Submits accountability report + photo. Conducts end-of-shift recaps. | Day 1 and Day 5 check-ins. Monitors submissions. |
| **Mentorship** | Days 6-30 | Available as a resource. Observes trainee on overlapping shifts. Notes regression or gaps. | Periodic spot-checks on trainee performance. |
| **Evaluation** | Day 30 | Written assessment of trainee readiness. | Conducts formal Day 30 evaluation. Pass/fail determination. |

### Day 30 Evaluation Outcomes

| Result | What It Means | Trainer Payout |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| **Pass** | Trainee is cleared. Ready for independent shifts. | Full incentive payout. |
| **Conditional** | 1-2 areas need improvement. Mentor period extended 2 weeks. | Partial payout now, remainder after re-eval. |
| **Fail** | Not ready. Root cause analysis: trainee issue or training execution issue? | Payout withheld pending review. |

# SECTION 9: THE BOTTOM LINE

**What we had:** Good content, inconsistent execution, no accountability system, no visibility.

**What we built:** 15 daily floor checklists, 3 enriched trainee manuals, 3 daily training guides, Google Sheets builders for professional printing, a content protocol, and a clear accountability structure.

**What changes for trainers:** Print the checklist. Follow it. Sign it. Photograph it. Submit the accountability report. Every shift. No shortcuts.

**What changes for trainees:** Real study material in Trainual. Knowledge checks that test substance, not recognition. A structured path from Day 1 to Day 30 evaluation.

**What changes for management:** Visibility into every training shift via the Accountability App. Day 1 and Day 5 check-ins. Day 30 formal evaluation. Trainer incentive tied to trainee outcomes.

**The system doesn't depend on one person. It runs because the process, the tools, and the accountability are built in. Same standard. Every trainer. Every location. Every time.**

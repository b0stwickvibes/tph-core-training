## TRAINING CHECKLIST

**Today's dynamic: Trainee runs the bar. Trainer shadows, observes, and coaches after, not during.**

### 1. Bar Takeover & Cleanliness Standards
- [ ] Trainee takes over the bar from the opening bartender. Evaluate the handoff: what's stocked, what's low, what needs attention.
- [ ] Assess and correct station condition: surfaces sanitized, tools clean, glassware polished, garnishes fresh
- [ ] Deep cleaning awareness: what gets done weekly/monthly, where supplies live, who's responsible
- [ ] A clean bar isn't a task. It's a standard. Maintain it throughout the entire shift.

### 2. High-Volume Service Execution
- [ ] Trainee runs the bar during live service. Trainer shadows.
- [ ] Speed techniques: pour efficiency, jigger accuracy at pace, building multiple drinks simultaneously
- [ ] Order prioritization: service well vs. bar guests, sequencing by build type, acknowledging the wait
- [ ] Workflow and movement: no wasted steps, station resets between orders, restocking during lulls
- [ ] This is the real thing. Show me you can handle volume without losing quality or composure.

### 3. Guest Interaction & Communication
- [ ] Trainee handles all guest interaction: greetings, orders, recommendations, complaints, close-outs
- [ ] Communication with team: calling out tickets, coordinating with servers, flagging issues to MOD
- [ ] Service standards in action: greeting within 30 seconds, check-back timing, professional farewell
- [ ] Trainer observes tone, confidence, and guest engagement. Coaches at natural breaks only.

### 4. Live Scenarios & Problem Solving
- [ ] Trainer creates real-time pressure: rapid-fire tickets, demanding guest role-play, curveball orders
- [ ] Trainee handles it. Trainer evaluates decision-making, composure, and recovery.
- [ ] "A guest says their drink is wrong." Trainee resolves it live. No stepping in unless safety is at risk.
- [ ] "A regular asks for something not on the menu." Trainee navigates it. Confidence over perfection.

### 5. POS Mastery
- [ ] Trainee handles all POS operations for the entire shift. No trainer assistance.
- [ ] Complex scenarios: split checks, large parties, comps/voids (with MOD approval), gift cards
- [ ] If the trainee still hesitates on Toast at this point, flag it. This must be resolved before mock service.

### 6. Closing Procedures
- [ ] Trainee leads the full closing process: breakdown, cleaning, restocking, cash-out, closing checklist
- [ ] Trainer observes and notes anything missed or done out of order
- [ ] Close the bar the way you'd want to find it when you open tomorrow.

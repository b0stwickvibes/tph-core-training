## TRAINING CHECKLIST

**Today's dynamic: Trainee runs the host stand. Trainer shadows, observes, and coaches after, not during.**

### 1. Autonomous Opening
- [ ] Trainee executes all pre-service tasks independently via Jolt app
- [ ] Host stand, iPads, reservation tools, menus ready without assistance
- [ ] Check in with MOD to confirm readiness. No trainer hand-holding.

### 2. Full-Service Host Execution
- [ ] Trainee manages all hosting duties from open to close: greetings, reservations, seating, phones, to-go, waitlist
- [ ] Handle walk-ins and reservations simultaneously during peak volume
- [ ] Manage server rotation, table pacing, and guest flow independently
- [ ] "This is the real thing. Show me you can run this stand without me."

### 3. Advanced Guest Relations
- [ ] Trainee handles all guest interaction: warm greetings, special requests, complaints, VIP recognition
- [ ] Strategic reservation management: balance walk-ins, reservations, and large parties through Resy
- [ ] Trainer observes confidence, composure, and problem-solving. Coaches at natural breaks only.

### 4. Team Synergy
- [ ] Communicate seamlessly with servers, food runners, kitchen, and management
- [ ] Announce parties, relay guest info, flag issues before they become problems
- [ ] "Proactive communication is what separates a good host from a great one."

### 5. End-of-Shift Procedures
- [ ] Trainee leads the full wrap-up: Jolt checklist, Resy updates, host stand reset, material restocking
- [ ] Final walkthrough of guest areas for cleanliness and security
- [ ] "Leave the stand the way you'd want to find it at the start of your next shift."

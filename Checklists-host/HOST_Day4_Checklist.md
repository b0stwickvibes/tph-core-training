## TRAINING CHECKLIST

### 1. Accelerated Setup
- [ ] Trainee executes full opening routine independently via Jolt app
- [ ] All tools, menus, iPads, and systems ready before service starts
- [ ] Trainer evaluates after. "By Day 4 this should be automatic."

### 2. Host Duties (Increased Independence)
- [ ] Trainee manages all host responsibilities: phones, reservations, to-go, seating, problem resolution
- [ ] Trainer shadows and coaches at natural breaks, not mid-task
- [ ] "Consistency is key. Repetition builds mastery."

### 3. Queue Optimization & Guest Flow
- [ ] Manage guest flow and wait times during peak service
- [ ] Communication and timing: keep guests informed, avoid empty tables or excessive delays
- [ ] Balance walk-ins with reservations strategically. Pacing is the host's primary lever.

### 4. Multitask Mastery
- [ ] Balance seating, phone calls, and to-go orders simultaneously
- [ ] Prioritize by time sensitivity and guest urgency
- [ ] Trainer increases pressure: simultaneous phone call + walk-in + server question
- [ ] "You don't need to be fast. You need to be smooth. Pace beats panic."

### 5. Cross-Functional Communication
- [ ] Practice clear, direct communication with servers, food runners, kitchen, and management
- [ ] Announce seated guests, notify of large parties, relay key guest info proactively
- [ ] "Great hosts prevent breakdowns by keeping everyone in the loop."

### 6. Shift Wrap-Up & Closing
- [ ] Complete closing checklist via Jolt. Finalize notes in Resy. Restock materials.
- [ ] Discuss performance with trainer and prep for Day 5 final evaluation
- [ ] "Tomorrow is prove-it day. Come ready to run the stand on your own."

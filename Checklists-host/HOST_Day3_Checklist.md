## TRAINING CHECKLIST

### 1. Host Duties (Full Shift Execution)
- [ ] Clock in, begin working through all core responsibilities with trainer
- [ ] Calls, reservations, to-go orders, seating, guest support
- [ ] Consistency in the basics builds reliability. Use every task to sharpen timing and organization.

### 2. Special Requests, Waitlists & Guest Concerns
- [ ] Work through real scenarios with trainer: dietary requests, unexpected parties, guest dissatisfaction
- [ ] Waitlist management: quote accurate wait times, communicate honestly, keep guests engaged
- [ ] "Stay poised and positive. Always communicate with servers or MOD when a concern escalates."

### 3. Problem Resolution & Rebuttals
- [ ] Practice responding to complaints calmly and quickly. De-escalation is your best tool.
- [ ] Review common rebuttal scripts from the Host Handbook. Practice with trainer.
- [ ] How to decline requests gracefully, redirect guests, and maintain professional consistency
- [ ] "Empathy and follow-up turn negative experiences around."

### 4. Shift Handover & End-of-Shift Standards
- [ ] How to properly brief the next host or MOD: wait times, upcoming reservations, unresolved concerns
- [ ] Maintain attentiveness and guest experience even during slower periods toward the end of your shift
- [ ] "Never drop standards at the end of a shift. Every guest deserves your best."

### 5. Venue Security & Closing
- [ ] Final walkthrough: guest areas secure, doors checked, bathrooms clear, report anything unusual to MOD
- [ ] Closing checklist via Jolt app: reset menus, tidy host stand, update Resy for tomorrow
- [ ] Check in with MOD, complete all tasks, review feedback before clocking out
- [ ] "A complete close supports the next day's success."
